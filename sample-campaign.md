# Campaign Brief: EcoTech Smart Home Launch

## Campaign Overview
Launch campaign for EcoTech's new line of AI-powered smart home devices that reduce energy consumption by up to 40%.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Tech-savvy homeowners aged 25-45 with household income $75K+
- **Secondary**: Environmentally conscious consumers interested in sustainability
- **Geographic**: Major metropolitan areas in US and Canada

## Key Messages
1. **Smart Energy Savings**: "Cut your energy bills in half with AI that learns your habits"
2. **Environmental Impact**: "Every EcoTech home prevents 2 tons of CO2 annually"
3. **Seamless Integration**: "Works with all major smart home platforms out of the box"

## Campaign Objectives
- Generate 50,000 qualified leads in Q1
- Achieve 15% market share in smart home energy category
- Build brand awareness to 25% among target demographic
- Drive $10M in pre-orders during launch period

## Creative Strategy
**Theme**: "Intelligence That Cares"
**Tone**: Innovative yet approachable, emphasizing both technology and environmental responsibility

### Key Creative Elements
- Hero product shots showcasing sleek design
- Real-time energy savings dashboards
- Customer testimonials with actual savings data
- Before/after home energy comparisons

## Media Strategy
**Budget**: $2.5M total campaign budget

### Channel Mix
- **Digital (60%)**: Social media, search, display advertising
- **Traditional (25%)**: TV spots during prime time, radio sponsorships
- **PR/Influencer (15%)**: Tech reviewers, sustainability advocates

### Timeline
- **Phase 1 (Weeks 1-2)**: Teaser campaign, influencer partnerships
- **Phase 2 (Weeks 3-6)**: Full launch, all channels active
- **Phase 3 (Weeks 7-8)**: Optimization and retargeting

## Success Metrics
- **Awareness**: Brand recall surveys, social mentions
- **Engagement**: Click-through rates, video completion rates
- **Conversion**: Lead quality scores, pre-order conversion rates
- **Sales**: Revenue attribution, customer acquisition cost

## Potential Risks
- **Competition**: Apple and Google may announce competing products
- **Technical**: Product delays could impact launch timing
- **Market**: Economic downturn affecting discretionary spending
- **Regulatory**: New energy efficiency standards could change landscape

## Budget Allocation
- Creative Development: $300K
- Media Buying: $1.8M
- Influencer Partnerships: $200K
- PR and Events: $150K
- Contingency: $50K

## Call to Action
"Reserve your EcoTech starter kit today and join the energy revolution. Limited early-bird pricing available."
